## medical revision and rebuttal gate

Medical revisions should fix manuscript truthfulness and auditability before adding decorative extra experiments.

When mapping reviewer comments, explicitly separate:

- wording / structure issues
- missing Methods detail
- unsupported claims that need downgrading
- truly necessary supplementary experiments

For medical prediction / modeling papers, rebuttal-ready deltas should keep these visible:

- complete Methods details suitable for clinicians and reviewers
- research-question-first Results wording instead of figure-by-figure narration
- operational definitions for any fancy method label
- endpoint provenance caveats when outcome semantics remain imperfect
- manuscript-safe reproducibility supplement updates
- removal of internal engineering names from manuscript-facing text
