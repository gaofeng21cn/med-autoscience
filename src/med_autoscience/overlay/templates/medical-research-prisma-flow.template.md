# PRISMA Flow Evidence Template

Purpose: capture transparent literature-screening or source-intake flow when a study, review, or manuscript claim needs PRISMA-style accounting. This is MAS-owned companion content, not a runtime, router, database, or screening authority.

MAS authority stays in source readiness records, evidence ledgers, claim-evidence maps, review ledgers, and publication evaluation. Use this template progress-first: preserve counts, exclusion reasons, and refs without turning screening into an unbounded search loop. Do not claim PRISMA compliance unless the required checklist and MAS reporting-guideline gate are present and passable.
